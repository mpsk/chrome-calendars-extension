# Chrome Web Store Description

## Short Description (Summary)
**Manage multiple Google Calendars in one place.**
View upcoming events from all your Google accounts in a single, unified list. Stay organized without switching tabs.

## Detailed Description
**Multi-Account Calendar** is the ultimate tool for managing your schedule across multiple Google accounts. Whether you have a work calendar, a personal calendar, and a freelance calendar, this extension brings them all together in one simple, clean interface.

**Key Features:**

*   **Unified Event List:** See all your upcoming events from different accounts in a single view.
*   **Multi-Account Support:** Connect as many Google accounts as you need.
*   **Smart Grouping:** Events are automatically grouped by "Today" and "Next" so you can focus on what's important right now.
*   **Quick Access:** Click any event to open it directly in Google Calendar.
*   **Privacy First:** Your data is safe. The extension communicates directly with Google's APIs and does not store your data on any third-party servers.

**Why install this extension?**
If you juggle multiple Google accounts, you know the pain of checking different tabs to see your full schedule. **Multi-Account Calendar** solves this by giving you a master view of your day, saving you time and ensuring you never miss a meeting again.
